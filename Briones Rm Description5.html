Lugaw, also spelled lugao, is a Filipino glutinous rice dish or porridge.

Lugaw may refer to various dishes, both savory and sweet. In Visayan regions, savory lugaw are

collectively referred to as pospas. Lugaw is widely regarded as a comfort food in the Philippines.
